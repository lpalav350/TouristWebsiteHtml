Features


Tourism Industry:

  1. Tourism is a temporary phenomenon.
  2. Tourist is a stranger to tourist place.
  3. It is movement of a person inside or outside any country.
  4. It is a movement outside home environment.
  5. Tourism is an activity selected by the choice of people.
  6. Tourism may be carried out for any reason but not for earning.
  7. There is no time limit. Tourism may be for a day or a stay of one month also.
  8. It is necessarily, a pleasure and recreational activity.



The tourism industry, also known as the travel industry, is linked to the idea of people travelling to other locations, either domestically or internationally, for leisure, social or business purposes. It is closely connected to the hotel industry, the hospitality industry and the transport industry, and much of it is based around keeping tourists happy, occupied and equipped with the things they need during their time away from home.
